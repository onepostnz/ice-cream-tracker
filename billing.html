Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
What happens after my trial ends?
After your 45-day free trial, you'll need to upgrade to continue using Ice Cream Tracker. All your data will be saved.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Absolutely! Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit cards via Stripe — Visa, Mastercard, American Express.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees! Just the monthly subscription price.
