Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
What happens after my trial ends?
After your 45-day free trial, you'll need to upgrade to a paid plan to continue using Ice Cream Tracker. All your data will be saved and ready when you upgrade.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment processor, Stripe.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees! Just pay the monthly subscription price for the plan you choose.
Do you offer annual billing?
Currently we offer monthly billing. Annual billing with a discount is coming soon!
